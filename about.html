About Us

Abc Education Australia is a leading study consultancy firm established in January’ 2017 in Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh, when Mohammad Shafiqur Rahman, Managing Director of Abc Education Australia started a small and innovative business for ‘helping’ Bangladeshi friends to study abroad, holiday work and traveling around Australia, New Zealand, Canada, UK, USA, and Europe OUR OBJECTIVE: The key objective of Abc Education Australia is to be an internationally recognized Education Consultancy firm who prepares students to “choose their right path” in pursuing higher studies abroad, in an ethical and professional manner. Our aim is to provide an excellent service at a reasonable cost, within agreed time scale. We aim to provide cost predictability wherever it is possible so that our student can prepare a budget for any given transaction. We do not believe that our student should be expected to pay disproportionately high charging rates for any matter. We therefore charge very competitive rates which reflect the complexity and the size of the matter involved. We believe in marinating the highest level of transparency while we work for you. We aim to provide tailor made solutions to cater to individual needs & meet their immigration objectives and to help them make more informed decisions. Our goal is to provide you with professional, competent & experienced services which will help you accomplish your personal study goals.
